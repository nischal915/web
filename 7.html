Information Security and Cyber Law

Information Security

Information security involves protecting digital data and systems from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. Key aspects include:

  • Encryption: Encoding data to protect it from unauthorized access.
  • Authentication: Verifying the identity of users or devices accessing a system.
  • Firewalls: Tools used to prevent unauthorized access to or from a network.

Cyber Law

Cyber law refers to legal issues related to the internet and digital technologies. It includes:

  • Privacy laws and data protection regulations.
  • Intellectual property rights in the digital environment.
  • Legal implications of cybercrimes like hacking and identity theft.
